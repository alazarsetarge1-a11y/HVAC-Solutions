Our Cool Story!

What started as three college students complaining about their broken AC in the middle of a Nashville summer has now turned into one of the city's most trusted heating and cooling companies. Alazar, Anusha, and Kayla had zero business experience, questionable life skills, and one shared dream: to never sweat through another August again. Fast forward 15 years and a few hundred thousand British Thermal Units later, CoolTech HVAC is still family-owned, still community-driven, and still showing up on time, which is more than we can say for the guys we used to call.
